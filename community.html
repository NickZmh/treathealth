We partner with community organizations.

Health care is local so we partner with local community-based organizations to address your social determinants of health. From food-insecurity to job-placement to family-support, we’re building relationships with organizations that want to help you and your family live better and be better.

Partnerships that are all about you.

Relationships that make a difference

Based on your circumstances, we get you in touch with organizations that have the resources for a better, happier, healthier you. Our relationships are meant to address socio-economic needs from housing to simple things like getting you the food you need from the local non-profit organization.

Easy access to community organizations

As we build stronger network of relationships across local organizations, we’ve made them accessible for you through our technology. We've always felt proper access to resources is the first to step to improvement. Your job shouldn’t be to scour around so we’ve done the research for you and would be even happier to further strengthen those relationships.
